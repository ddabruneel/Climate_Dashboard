Since 1990, 43 mainly developed countries agreed to submit a detailed annual inventory of their greenhouse gas emission.

Reliable, complete and detailed datasets about climate change are still not readily available. The premise of this dashboard is to give everyone an opportunity to "play with the data", comparing historical consumption by country, by sector, sub-sector, etc.

The dataset presented here is an extraction from the official UN organization, UNFCCC website: https://di.unfccc.int/time_series

The extractor used comes from this team: Data Package with UNFCCC emissions data
